# Getting Support

Browse the [help center](https://staylime.zendesk.com/hc/en-us) or submit a ticket via our [support form](https://staylime.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/requests/new). We respond within 1–2 business days.

---

## Support hours

Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM Central European Time. No weekend email support.

---

## How to get a faster response

- After submitting the form, check your inbox for an automated reply. No reply within a few minutes means we didn't receive your message — check your spam folder and verify your email address.
- Don't send follow-up emails asking for a status update — it moves your ticket to the end of the queue.
- For general Shopify questions (products, collections, pages, checkout), contact [Shopify support](https://help.shopify.com/en/questions) directly — they're available 24/7.
- If Shopify already contacted us on your behalf, no need to reach out separately — we've received it.

---

## What's covered

- Questions about theme and app functionality
- Help with initial setup and configuration
- Fixing bugs in our products

For questions about third-party apps or custom code not from Staylime, contact the respective developer directly.

---

## Why no live chat or phone support?

Our ticket system ensures every inquiry is handled in order — no customer waits longer than another. For technical issues it also lets us share screenshots, screen recordings, and code snippets to give precise answers.
